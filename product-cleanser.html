Description

Cloud Cleanse keeps extensions hygienic and fresh, which is critical for long retention. Safe for daily retail sales to clients and back-bar preparation. Vegan-friendly and ophthalmologist tested.

  • Ingredients: Tea Tree Extract, Rose Water distillate, Decyl Glucoside
  • Formula: Foam Pump
  • Volume: 60ml
  • Accreditations: Paraben-Free, Latex-Free, Cruelty-Free
How to Use

Pump a small cloud of foam onto the styling lash brush. Close your eyes and gently sweep the foam downward through the lash extensions and along the lid margin. Rinse thoroughly with cool water and pat dry with a lint-free towel. Brush extensions back to shape.
