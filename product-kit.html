How to Use

Cleanse eyelashes, apply silicone shields to the eyelids with adhesive, comb natural lashes straight up onto the shield. Apply Step 1 (Lift) for 8-10 minutes. Remove and apply Step 2 (Set) for 8-10 minutes. Clean off solutions and shields, apply moisturising lash serum oil to finish.
