Get salon-quality results at home in under 10 minutes. The Lash Crush Instant Kit contains lightweight individual under-lash clusters, a dual-ended bond and seal glue, and professional applicator tweezers. Lasts up to 7 days.

Description

Designed for beauty lovers on the go. The under-lash application method creates a seamless, invisible lash line. Clusters are reusable up to 3 times with proper care and cleaning.

  • Lash Quantity: 36 clusters (mixed lengths: 10mm, 12mm, 14mm)
  • Bond & Seal Glue: 5ml dual tube (latex-free)
  • Wear Duration: 5 – 7 days
  • Tweezers: Curved applicator style
How to Use

Brush a thin layer of Bond under your natural eyelashes. Pick up a cluster using the applicator tweezers, placing it underneath your natural lashes (1mm away from the waterline). Press gently to secure. Apply a thin layer of Seal on top to lock in placement and remove sticky residues.
