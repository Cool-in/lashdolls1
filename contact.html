About Us

Our Story

LashDolls Dublin was founded in 2016 by Karen Grehan, a passionate beauty industry educator, entrepreneur, and mentor with over a decade of hands-on experience.

What began as a dedicated local salon in Ratoath, Co. Meath, has evolved into one of Ireland's most respected beauty brands and training academies. Over the years, LashDolls has trained and mentored hundreds of beauty professionals, helping them gain the technical skills and business acumen to launch their own successful careers.

"My mission has always been simple: to empower aspiring beauty artists with the skills, confidence, and ongoing mentorship to build their own thriving independent businesses."

When salon closures hit during the pandemic, Karen pivoted the business model to focus on supply and digital learning. Today, LashDolls is proud to be a major Irish distributor of professional-grade lash and brow products, running a physical salon alongside its high-growth training academy.

Our core philosophy remains the same: empowering students with the skills, confidence, and ongoing mentorship needed to turn their passion into a thriving independent business.

Karen Grehan
Founder & Lead Educator
