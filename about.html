About the Salon

Discover the passion, training, and philosophy that fuels the LashDolls aesthetic experience.

Artistry & Mentorship

The heart of custom eye aesthetics

"We strive to combine precision aesthetics with supportive education."

Founded as a high-end lash haven, LashDolls Dublin was built to elevate individual client styling. Our salon in Ratoath, Co. Meath, features clean, tranquil, and modern facilities where clients receive custom-tailored lash sets and brow restructuring services.

We are dedicated to safety and quality. That's why we formulate our own professional retention adhesives, source cashmere extensions, and use top-tier lamination products.

Our academy represents a continuation of this high standard. By hosting CPD & ABT accredited workshops, we train new student artists in theoretical lash anatomy, practical fan isolation, and client business scaling.

Advanced Hygiene Standards

Strict tool sanitisation and clean client environments for complete peace of mind.

Ongoing Student Support

Academy students receive lifetime access to mentorship, business tips, and pro product discounts.
